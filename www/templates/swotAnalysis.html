SWOT Analysis

Strength

1. Does your company have technological advantages?
2. Do you perform better than your competitiors?
3. Are you strategically well placed within your sector?
4. Do you have features that your customers perceive as your strength?
5. Does your company have factors that faciliate possible sales?

Weakness

1. Are the activities performed by your company weaker than required?
2. Do you waste your resources which are your strength?
3. Does your company have factors than customer perceive as weakness?
4. Do you think your product/service can be improved?
5. Does you perform unncecessary activites that could be avoided?

Opportunities

1. Does your company have good business opportunities?
2. Are there needs that your clients have but are not offered by your competitors?
3. Is there scope for technological development in the market?
4. Is there a possibility for political or legal changes in the market?
5. Is there scope for other changes that will favour your range of products?

Threats

1. Are there tendencies that could menace the exitence of your company/product/services?
2. Are your competitors improving the products/services better than you?
3. Do you face obstacles in the functioning of your company?
4. Does technological changes threaten your company's position?
5. Do you face issues regarding capital resources?
